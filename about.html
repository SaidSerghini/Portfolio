Just me.

I'm a 23 year old student. I was born in Antwerp, Belgium on 20 september 1991. In my free time i love to play futsal(indoor football) and like to spend time with my cousins.

For me design is an amasing thing you can find in everything. It's just something amazing that makes life just more pleasant. Personally I prefer flat design, because i like the way that everything is in the right place and has a logically lay-out.

It's just amazing how you can start from zero and begin your research to finally achieve and design something unique, somthing you can call yours.
